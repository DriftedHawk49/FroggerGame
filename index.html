Frogger

An Endless Game in which you have to make your player cross the stone blocks Without Touching any Bug !
If you collide with a Bug, it will kill you and your Wins become 0!.
More number of times your player makes it across the stone path, Your Wins keep on Increasing !

Move UP Move DOWN Move LEFT Move RIGHT Adds a Life. Maximum 3 Blue Gem. 100 Points Green Gem. 200 Points Orange Gem. 500 Points Rock. Obviously to Block You.
